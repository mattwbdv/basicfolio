FaceFinder Web App

As part of a React.js course, I created a FaceFinder web application. The app leverages the Clarifai API to detect and identify all faces in photographs uploaded by the user. The app then draws a box around each face. The app keeps a database of users, the number of photos they have uploaded, and their contact information along with secure (encrypted) credentials.
